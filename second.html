Difference of Champions:

There are 5 different types of champions in League of Legends: Support, Tank, Assassin, Marksman and Mage. The goal of the Tank is to soak as much damage as possible from all other champions. Much like a heavy duty tank used in the army. The Assassin sneaks his way behind the lower health champions to assassinate them before they can do anything to anyone. The marksman is usually a range champion that damages other champions from afar. The support is mainly used to support the marksman that generally has low health and the main objective of the mage is to cause as much chaos as possible disorienting the enemy team.

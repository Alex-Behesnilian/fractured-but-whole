What is League of Legends?

League of Legends is an MMORPG (Massive Multiplayer Online Role Play Game). The game consists of two teams (Purple and Blue). Each team consists of 5 players. Each player select one unique champion that has a specific set of skills. The goal of the game is to destroy the enemy teams Nexus.

Why I chose League of Legends:

I started playing League of Legends during the summer with a few of my friends as it is listed one of the top MMORPG games today. I think its a really fun and strategic game to play that doesn't require too much effort. The more you play the more you want to play as you get better and better.
